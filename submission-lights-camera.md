### Design Choices

I use a binderHelper method to factor out my binding calls. In addition, I use global variables to create a template for each shape. This allows me to just pass in my ctm into my shader. In addition, I have a camera class that handles the projMat calculations and update calls. I have different shape classes for tesselation.

### Known Bugs

My specular components are a bit off. This was not the case before as I remember mapping my specular light pos with the outputs. To fix this, I tried debugging the light directions extensively and used print statements to check the values I was passing in. In addition, I tried very hard to fix the normals of my cone but couldn't come to an appropriate solution. My approach at present was to hard code the value for the tip. I do so by averaging out the normals from the surrounding vertices. The final result does seem correct but when I integrate it with my phong the specular component becomes off.

For all these bugs I extensively used a debugger and kept a track of my uniforms on paper.
